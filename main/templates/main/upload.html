How To Use

1. Place a white piece of printer paper or a white tray on a flat surface.
2. Check to make sure that you are in a well lit area.
3. Disperse coins on surface as far apart from each other as possible. Avoid overlapping.
4. Tap "Choose File" to either take a picture using your mobile phone or select a picture from your files. Take a picture as close as possible to the coins while still leaving white space around the edges.
5. Press "Submit" and your result should appear instantly.
